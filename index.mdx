Whether you want to create a LoRA for a specific art style, run DreamBooth to teach the model a new subject, or fine-tune an entire model, Kohya's GUI wraps Kohya's powerful training scripts in an accessible Gradio interface — no command-line expertise required.

## Get started in minutes

<Steps>
  <Step title="Choose your installation method">
    Pick the setup that fits your platform. For most users, the `uv`-based launcher is the fastest path to a working environment. See the [installation overview](/installation/overview) for all options.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Launch the GUI">
    Run `./gui-uv.sh` on Linux/macOS or `gui-uv.bat` on Windows. The Gradio interface will open in your browser at `http://127.0.0.1:7860`.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure your dataset">
    Prepare your training images in a folder, add captions if needed, and point the GUI to your dataset directory. See [dataset preparation](/configuration/dataset-preparation) for details.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Start training">
    Select your training method tab (LoRA, DreamBooth, etc.), fill in your model and output paths, then click **Start Training**. Monitor progress via TensorBoard or WandB.
  </Step>
</Steps>
